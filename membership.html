MEMBERSHIP INFORMATION
Sanctory operates on an invitation-only basis. Occasionally, we allow referrals from existing members to invite friends and family. This quiet, considered approach allows us to plan demand in a way that aligns with the natural pace of our producers, many of whom work with traditional, small-scale methods. By doing so, we ensure that quality remains consistent across every batch and each member receives a product that is genuinely special, without any compromise.
Currently, new members are accepted through invitations extended by existing members or during specific open enrollment periods announced by Sanctory. You can register your interest on our site, and we’ll notify you if new spots become available.
Our selective approach helps us maintain a high standard of curation and quality. It allows producers to focus on craftsmanship rather than volume and ensures each member experiences exclusivity and uniqueness in every product.
Sanctory offers a range of artisanal goods including fine foods, luxury wellness items, handcrafted lifestyle products, and rare seasonal editions—all carefully sourced from master producers around the world.
Sanctory products reflect the craftsmanship, scarcity, and ethical practices behind their creation. While prices vary, our focus is always on fair compensation for producers and delivering unmatched quality to members.
